About Spain

Spain is a country located in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula. It is known for its rich history and culture, vibrant cities, beautiful beaches, delicious cuisine, and lively festivals. Some of the most famous cities in Spain include Madrid, Barcelona, Seville, Valencia, and Granada.

The official language of Spain is Spanish, which is spoken by the majority of the population. However, there are also regional languages such as Catalan, Galician, and Basque, which are spoken in different parts of the country.

Spain has a Mediterranean climate with hot summers and mild winters, but the northern regions have a cooler, more temperate climate. It is home to several famous landmarks, including the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona, the Alhambra in Granada, and the Prado Museum in Madrid.
